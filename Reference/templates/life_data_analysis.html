Disposition table requirements

WO and PM disposition tables display name, taskID, createdDate_Final, completedDate_Final, Weibull event date/source, downtime, completionNotes, requestTitle, requestorDescription, and disposition notes. Saves write current/historical rows to event_disposition.

PM records are never counted as failures; PMs only become Weibull-usable with INCLUDED_PM_RESET_EVENT and APPROVED_RESET. Corrective WOs only become Weibull-usable with INCLUDED_FAILURE.
